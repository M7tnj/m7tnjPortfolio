The Journey

"What is the lasting impact of my work?"

This question led me to the intersection of Computer Science and Biology. While I initially viewed these fields as distinct paths, the Corona Pandemic became a time of self-discovery. Through thousands of hours of dedicated study, I connected the dots and discovered the key: Bioinformatics.

My education extended far beyond the classroom. While mastering R programming for microarray data, I realized technical skill alone was insufficient without understanding the clinical logic. I pushed myself into the clinical world of cancer and oncology, participating in tumor board examinations and earning CME credits to bridge the gap between code and cure.
